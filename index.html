Growin' Noobs

Gameplay

You are a small broke bozo who needs to get money to buy some brand new [insert item here]. You were given some noob seeds to plant and once they grow big enough noob heads, you sell the noob heads for money. There are different rarities of seeds and plants along with different types and sizes depending on RNG. You start your noob head empire by selling enough noob heads.

Storyline

You're a failure and broke
